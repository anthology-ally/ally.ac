    Your project manager will contact you to schedule a kick-off call. We encourage you to start thinking about desired timeline and roll-out strategy prior to this call.

    A technical consultant will provide full support to set up the Ally integration with your LMS.

    This training session will provide a detailed overview of Ally’s features and the integration into your LMS.

  • Institutional Report training session

    The second training session will provide an in-depth review of your institutional report data.
