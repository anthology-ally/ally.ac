Making course content accessible

Blackboard Ally is a revolutionary product that focuses on making digital course content more accessible. Using inclusivity, sustainability, and automation as its key pillars, Blackboard Ally helps you understand and tackle accessibility in a way that benefits all students.

Alternative accessible formats

Automatically run course materials through an accessibility checklist. Using advanced Machine Learning algorithms, you can generate a range of more accessible alternatives for the instructor's original content including Semantic HTML, audio, ePub, and electronic Braille.

Instructor Feedback

Provide the instructor with feedback about the accessibility of their course materials and guidance on how to fix identified accessibility issues. Increase awareness and understanding of accessibility with instructors and generate a change in behavior over time.

Institutional Reporting

Receive an institution-wide course content accessibility report that allows for deep insight and understanding into how your institution is performing from a course content accessibility point of view. Use it to track progress and identify problem areas to inform decisions.
